2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account Data (Business Clients)

  • Business name, type, address, phone, website
  • Owner email address and password (encrypted)
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe - we never store card details)
  • Business description, FAQ content, opening hours

2.2 Conversation Data

  • Messages exchanged between your customers and the AI assistant
  • Booking details provided by customers (name, phone, date, time)
  • Language preferences of customers

2.3 Technical Data

  • IP addresses (anonymized after 30 days)

  • Deployment and error logs (retained for 7 days)

3. Why We Process Your Data

  • Contract performance: To provide the AI assistant service you subscribed to
  • Legitimate interest: To improve service quality and fix technical issues
  • Legal obligation: To comply with Luxembourg and EU tax and legal requirements
  • Consent: For marketing communications (you can withdraw at any time)

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored and processed within the European Union:

  • Website hosting: Vercel (EU infrastructure)
  • Email delivery: Resend (Ireland, eu-west-1)
  • Payment processing: Stripe (EU servers, PCI-DSS compliant)
  • AI processing: Anthropic API (encrypted in transit)

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS 1.3, AES-256) for all data in transit and at rest.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your subscription + 12 months
  • Conversation data: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Payment records: Retained for 10 years (Luxembourg tax law requirement)
  • Technical logs: Retained for 7 days

  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
